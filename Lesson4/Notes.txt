Network:
	A group of entities that can communicate, even 
	though they are not all directly connected

Latency:
	Time it takes message to get from source to 
	destination. From the time source start messaging
	to the time the destination starts receiving.
	It's measure in milliseconds.

Bandwith:
	Amount of information that can be transmitted per
	unit time.
	It's measure in bit/second or Mbps 

Bit:
	Smallest unit of information
